About Me


“Kofi” Matthew Zlatnik is an aspiring web developer and student in the University of Oregon’s Web Development Boot Camp. Kofi has a broad range of professional experiences, including facilities management, contracting in commercial lighting, operations analysis, and financial services.

He has long desired to add software programming to his skill set and pursue a specialized career that will benefit from his analytical mind and creative instincts.

Kofi has a bachelor’s degree from George Fox University (2006, Magna Cum Laude) in International Business. He acquired the moniker “Kofi” in Ghana, West Africa, where he lived in 2001-02.
